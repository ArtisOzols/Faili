Grot.
Leelā peektdeenā.
1. Kungs, Tu effi grehkus neffis, Mannu grehku parradus, Effi grehku-lahstus zeetis, Effi elles mohkas grimmis; Tas man firdi laustin lausch.
2. Elies-dahrsa assins-smcedri Pluhst no Tawa waidsiņa; Tawa sirds irr noskumrnufi Lihds pat pasuschanas-nahmei; Tas man firdi laustin lausch.
3. Krusta-kohka: „Eli! Eli!" Brehz ta sirds ar drebbeschan'; M an ni grehki Temi spaida. Un ar elles mohkahm baida; Tas man sirdi laustin lausch.
4. Ak ko buhs man Temim eedoht, Kungs, par Tamu zeeschanu? Masu leetu es Tem dohdu: Maunu firdi Tem atnestu; Nemm scho mahjn uppuru.
