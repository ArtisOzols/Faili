Tam Kungam peederam.
Tam Kungam peederam, lai dsihwojam jeb nomirstam. Tam Kungam peederam, kaS reis preeksch wişeem miriS; Tam Kuugam peederam, zaur Wiņu misu mantojam. Tam Kungam peederam, kaS misu mumS ir dewis.
2.
Tam Kungam peederam; tad lai ar' Wiņam dsihwojam Uu Wiņa, Wiņa weeu ar mceş' un dwehş'li esam, — Arween ar şaweem darbcem, şawu muti leezinam, Ka teescham mehS to Kungu sawâ şirdî nesam.
3.
Tam Kungam peederam. un tamdehļ paşaul'S tumşibâ Mums naw wiS jabaidahs. jo mumS şpihd swaigsne gaifcha. Un ta ar şaweem stareem mihligeem muhS preezina; Schi swaigsne ir tas dahrgaiS wahrds, ka mehs ta Kunga.'
4.
Tam Kungam peederam; Winşch zihuişchanä beidsamâ. Kur zita palihdsiba tahļu, mumS to neşihS. Un şwehtn preeku, drofchibu dos nahweS stundiņā. Tam Kungam peederam, un Wiņşch pee şew muhs wedihs.
Alma Ulter.
