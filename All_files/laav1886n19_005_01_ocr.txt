Lehceju mihklas uzminejums.
(Şkat. 17. nummurâ.)
Lai buhtu kungs, jeb kalps, jeb neeks, Jeb semneeks, kopman's, amatneeks, Pats mahzitajs, jeb mahzetlis, Jeb Luters, luhds. jeb Katolis, — Taws wahrds, Deews, to ikkatram rahd', Ko dariht buhs, preekşch azihm stahd'. Kas şirşnigi tam paklauşa, Ir şwehtigs şcheit un muhschibâ.
