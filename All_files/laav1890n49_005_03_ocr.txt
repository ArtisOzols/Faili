Dazchadi sihkumi.
Kà war ahbolus weegli nomisot. Ahbolus, kurus waijaga eewahrit waj kukņâ isleetat, war wisdrihsaki no misas atşwabinat, kad tos us kahdeem azumirkļeem eeleek wahroşchâ uhdenî. Misu tad war nowilkt it kà plahnu ahdu. Pirmkahrt zaur to teek laiks aistaupits, un otrkahrt uekas nepasuhd no wehrtigahs augļa gaļas. Preeksch ahboleem, kurus negrib wahrit, şchahda rihkoşchanahs naw eewehlama. Katrs, kas sina, kahda garşcha ahbolam, kuŗşch gulējis tikai ihşu laiziņu zepjamâ krahşnî, to weegļi şapratihs. Fr. 81.
