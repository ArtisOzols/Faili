Sirds, paceetees!
1.
Sirds, pazeetees, kad netaisni tew jazeesch, Kad nepareisibas tew japaness; Nenoskumstees, kad behdas tewi nospeesch, Taws Tehlvs, kas augscha, temi ne-atstahs.
2.
Tu apsinees, ka nemainiga efi, Nekahdu ļaunu nesi darij'si. Tik pazeetigi wisu, wisu nest, Pazeeschana nekad naw mihlufi.
3.
Pehz ruhktahm behdahm preeki temi gaidihs, Pehz leetus mihļa saulit' atspihdehs; Tad lihgsmiba tem misur preti smaidihs, Un tamas behdas fimtkahrt atmaksahs.
4.
Tamdehļ us augscheeni tem ween buhs skatiht, No kurenes tem sargs un palihgs nahk. Dauds labāk netaisnibu zeest, ka dariht, — Tem palihdseht Deems ihsta laika mahk.
M— a Ezermaleete.
