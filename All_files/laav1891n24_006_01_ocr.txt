.Gods lai ir tanī Tevtvam un tam Dķhlam un tam Swebtam Garam!"

Reis smehta nakts pahr kluso semi nahza. Kur gaischums sposchs is tumsas rahdijahs: Deems suhtija scho gaismu semê zilmekeem. Tad debess draudsiba ar preeku augstu gamileja: Şwehts muhsu Deems ir! Minam gods! GodS lai ir tam Tehmam! 2.
Reis rihts, tik smehts. pee semeS debess ausa. Kur gaischums sposchs is tumsas raifijahS, — Jo elles stabi luhsa, kapa wahrti atmehrahs. Tad debess draudsiba ar preeku augstu gamileja: Şwehts muhsu Deems! Lai gods tam Tehmam Un tam Dchlam!
