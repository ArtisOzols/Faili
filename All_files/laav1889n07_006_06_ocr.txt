Frantşchu waldiba pahr to şuhdseja pee Kreemn maldibas, kas nu tur aisşuhtihs wirşneeku ar pawehli. wişus Kreemu pawalstneekus no turenes ar kuģi pahrwest atpakaļ us Krewiju.
