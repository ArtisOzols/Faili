Peemini man', pee altāra kad stahmi. Un zitam samu rohku atMehle; Tad atmini, ka meens sew mehlahs nahmi, Kam mairs nam preeka schini pasaule.
Peemini man', kad pirmohs preekus baudi. Kad juhtees semi ļohti laimiga; Tad atmini, ka Meenu reis it smehti Tu sohlijees buht mana mihļaka.
Peemini man', kad firds tem Preeka dohdahs. Kad gara mehl mans maigs tem parahdahs; Tad atmini, ka meena firds mehl rohdahs. Kas klusuma pehz tem raud asaras.
Peemini man', kad skumjas behdas krihti, Kas schini muhscha beeschi atgadahs; Tad atmini, kur nemainiba glihti Pee manas kruhts Tu dauds reis peeglaudees.
Peemini man', kad firds tem skumjas seenahs. Kad dsihmes zeļu redsi tumschu gan; Tad atmini tahs jaukahs laimes deenas, Kur saldu mihlibu mehs baudijahm.
Peemini man', kad nomiris es buhschu. Un guļdihts dsestra semes klehpiti; Tad atmini, ko beidsoht temim luhdsu: Stahd' mirs' us mana kapa — rohsiti!
Pehdigā wehleschanahs.
