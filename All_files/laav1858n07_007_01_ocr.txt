Kahds zeetumnecks, kad mahzitajs Minnu us grehku noschehloschanu skubbinaja. tam atbildēja: „Es esmu pamiffam pasuddis!" — „Nu, tas man preeks, kad tu effi pasuddis," eeesauzahs ahtri mahzitajs. „Ka tas jums par preeku marr buht?" Praffija zeetumneeks. „Ja," teize mahzitajs, „par to no miffas sirds preezajohs tadehļ: ka lesus Kristus irr pasaule nahzis, tohs kas pasudduschi, mekleht. Nu miņsch temi arri atraddihs."
