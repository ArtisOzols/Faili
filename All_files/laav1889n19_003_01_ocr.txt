Mans dehls. kad domà dsihwi şahkt, Buhs pasemibas şkolâ nahkt. Eeksch şirds un gara semojees. Ar darbeem nekad lepojees. Tad tu no Deema şwehtihts buhş'. No labeem ļaudihm mihlehts ļļuhf. Un wişa tawa maloda Lai buht' ar şahli bahrstita. Ar mişeem laipni runādams. Lai bagāts, nabags, kahds buhdams. Pret wişeem tureht lehnu praht'. Ar tumak' nekad teeşâ staht. Tos pateeşibas şwahrkus tew No Deema puşes pirz preekşch şew. Ar teem tu meenmehr puzejees. Ar labeem ļaudihm pulzejees. Neleelees ta. kà Goliāts, Ko semê kahwa Dahmids kahds. Ko wahrdôs esi şotijees. To darbôs labpraht ispildees. Ar taişnu mehr' un şwaru şwer. Labāk ar kaudsi katram ber. Tad, şakratihts un peeşpaidihts, Tams muhscha mehrs buhs peepildihts. Ikweenam şawu godu dod, Kas mahza, malda, maj dod şod'. Pehz zita laimes netihko, Pee darbeem muhscham neslinko. Ar smeedreem kas ir nopelnihts. Lai top no temim pataupihts. Un dehls. kad doma seemu ņemt, No dabas tad tew to buhs lemt. Nemeklē to eeksch sihdes ģehrbtu. Bet tihrâs drahnâs, paschas wehrptu. Nemeklē to eeksch augsteem radeem. Kam manta kraht' no gadu aadeem. Mihļsch dehls lihds kapa malmai Tu taisnigs isturees, — Tad buhs tew muhscha wakarâ. Ar asarahm kas pawada. Tad behrni tew pee kapa nahks. Ar asarahm tew' apbehrt sahks.
