I. B.
Ak şwehtais Gars. eepreezatajs, Ar şwehtu Garu peepildi
Tu ihstais şirds apşkaidrotajs, Muhş' wahjas şirdis schehligi. —
Un wadons muhşu kahjahm, Taws Gars lai muhs ween wada;
Wed muhs pee ihstas tizibas. Tas lai mums prahtâ peemahjo,
Un tawa wahrda gaişchibas, — Ik deeniņas muhs apgaişmo,
Dod palihgu tam wahjam. Mums jaunas şirdis rada.
Nahz tu, Ješu. Şwehtu preeku,
Mums palihdsi, ka ik brihdi Us tew tizeht, tew peedereht,
Tawam Garam Neşpehzibâ,
Wişâs weetâs klauşiht waram. Lai dod tawa schehlastiba.
Neweens kà ween tas şwehtais Gars Uswar to paşaul', ka nemeers Pee mums nekad, wairs zelşees; Wiņşch Deewu bihtees mahza muhs, Pee ta ween nahk, kas şchķihsti buhs, — No grehkeem paşargajees. Lai mehs tew', Tehws, Şcheitan teizam un nebeidsam Arween şlaweht, —
Muhscham taişni preekşch tew stahweht!
Wasaras-swehtkōs.
