Zik leela, roaļdon', Tawa Marra,
To nespehj muhsu prahti roeikt;
Ko Tawa rohka, spehka darra —
Kas to marr isprast! slaroeht beigt!!
Ais firmas muhschibas Mehl slamehs,
Tew, Tamus darbus pasaule;
