Jezus, manna saule, raddi zeedus eeksch man.
Meld. Nu dussehs tvissaS leetas. 1.
Ta saul' ar karstu spohschum'. Mans Pestitajs ar kohschum
Eeksch mannu sirdi spihd;
No saules seedi zellahs,
No lesus dmehsel' smellahs
Tohs seedus, kas ne muhscham kriht.
2.
Tee seed' irr pasemmiba,
Tee seed' irr mihlestiba,
Tee seed' irr zerriba,
No tizzibas tee issell.
Un puķķu-kohschum' uszell
Turp, kur seed muhscham lihgsmiba.
3.
Kungs Ješu, es to mcenu Tew luhdsu nakt' un deenu: Dohd' man to tizzibu, Kas no Tew auglib' issmell Un smehtos seedos eesell. Un manto debbess-dsihrvibu.
4.
