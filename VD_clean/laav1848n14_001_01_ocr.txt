Us Awizchu lassitajeem.
Kà us tahļu şweşchu semmi aisgahjuşcha drauga no turrenes atlaista grahmata wiuna draugeem jo leelu preeku darra, tà zerreju, ka ir şchi appakşchâ stahwedama dſeesmiņa tahs preekẜch pahri mehnescheem pee Deerva aisgahzuşchas basnizkunga-atraitnes Mûller, ko ẜtarp wiunas atlikkuscheem raksteem eẜmu atraddis, wişşeem wiunas pasihstameem, ar kuŗŗeem ta daschureis schinms lappiņas irr sarunnajusees, teem daſchu labbu mahzibu dohdama, buhs kà rveena jo patihkarna atflauna no debbesim, un to tadehļ eeleeku şchinnîs awisês.
