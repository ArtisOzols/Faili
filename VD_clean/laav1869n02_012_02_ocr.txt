Krusts.
Kad mums wirs semmes uekahda krusta nebuhtu, bet preeki meen', tad par geķķeem paliktum un dohmatum, ẜchi semme essoht debbesis.
Mehs tad arri nesaprastum to: „Eli, Eli, lama asaphtani? Mans Deews, mans Deervs, kam manni effi atstahjis?"
Un waj tad tas neklahjahs, ka tee lohzekļi bauda to ettiķi, ar ko miunu Galma irr dſirdinata? *
