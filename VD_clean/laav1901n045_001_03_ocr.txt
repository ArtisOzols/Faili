Paspahrnē.
Acis turpu ween greezchās,
Projām kur pagahtne grimst; Şimtukahrt bijuşchàs şahpes Kruhtîs no jauua dſimst.
Paşlehpees. pagahtne şkumjà, Gana jaìl ilgi man zeest, — Ko tu man aiswedi — muhscham Şpehjams naw atpakaļ greest!
Nè, zaur tagadnes tumşu Jaunai gaismai buhs pluhst. Kura lai şadseedè mainas, Kuŗâ lai asaras schuhst!
A. Sauleets.
