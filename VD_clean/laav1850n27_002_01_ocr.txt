Gudra pelle.
No ohrgas pelle isschahmahs Un eeraudsija lammatas. „AH re!"—ta teiza,—„walgs kur ẜtahw! Tas pellehm uszelts tur par nahw'. Ak tarvus gudrus zilmekus! Şkatt', tà ẜchee dohma guhstiht muhs.
Ah ha!— mums gudrahm pellehm jau Şchi flunfte senn, senn galma ẜtahw: Ja kahdai prahts tur peclihst klaht, Uu kahrs to spekķi kustiuaht, Ta klaps! tas akmins kriht un mai! Gals klaht us reiſ tur nabbagai.
Bet pa-ohstiht, — to marru gan, Ne ko tas ļauna ne kaisch man; Tamdehļ tas akmins ne warr krist, Nedſ gudru pelli semmê sist. Man speķkis tihk par warru ohst, — To marru. Tikkai ne buhs kohst."—
Nu lammatas ta eelihda, Un şpeķķa-şmakku ohstija. Ak, kahda gahrda.'— Şeekalas Tai milkahs, — deggons peeduhrahs Pee speķķa. Klaps! malgs sakritta, Un kahrumneezi nositta.
Kas leelitees prett grehkeem ẜahk, Ka wiņẜch ta miltus ſinn un mahk, Un warr, lai ne ſinn kahrums kahdſ. To ohstiht — jo tam essoht prahts; Tam tà kà pellei notiks drihſ: To grehku – malgi nosittihs! —
E. Dunsberg.
