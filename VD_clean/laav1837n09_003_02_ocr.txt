Swehtdeenas darbs.
I.
Man şwehdeenâ eijoht us basuizu Kahdſ uabbags wihrs luhdse man dahwanu; To labpraht tam dewu kà peenahkahs, Lai şwehdeenâ ne raud, — bet preezajahs.
Un brihiiums! — us weetas man likkahs tà Ka buhtu jau bijis es basniza! — Tomehr tik nule turp gribbeju eet —! Labs darbs ka basniza! tà mau nu schķeet.
