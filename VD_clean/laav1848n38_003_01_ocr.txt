Kas darrams, kad kas sadedzinajees.
Kad tewim kahda rveeta pee meeşas şadedsinata, tad aptraipi to tuhliht pehz sadedsinaşchanas ar elji, un eerihrve to ar sahli, tad drihſ wişşas sahpes tervim mitteftes. un pehz kahdas puşş stundiņas tarva ahda atkal buhs kà bijufe, beſ ka tervim kahda tulsna tur zeltohs, jeb ahda noeetu, jeb wainas tewim paliktu.
