Peemini man', pee altāra kad stahmi. Un zitam ẜawu rohku atMehle; Tad atmini, ka weens sew mehlahs nahmi, Kam wairs naw preeka schini pasaule.
Peemini man', kad pirmohs preekus baudi. Kad juhtees semi ļohti laimiga; Tad atmini, ka Weenu reiſ it smehti Tu sohlijees buht mana mihļaka.
Peemini man', kad firdſ tem Preeka dohdahs. Kad gara wehl mans maigs tem parahdahs; Tad atmini, ka weena firdſ wehl rohdahs. Kas klusuma pehz tem raud asaras.
Peemini man', kad skumjas behdas krihti, Kas schini muhscha beeschi atgadahs; Tad atmini, kur nemainiba glihti Pee manas kruhts Tu daudſ reiſ peeglaudees.
Peemini man', kad firdſ tem skumjas seenahs. Kad dſihmes zeļu redſi tumschu gan; Tad atmini tahs jaukahs laimes deenas, Kur saldu mihlibu mehs baudijahm.
Peemini man', kad nomiris es buhschu. Un guļdihts dſestra semes klehpiti; Tad atmini, ko beidsoht temim luhdſu: Stahd' mirs' us mana kapa — rohsiti!
Pehdigā wehleschanahs.
